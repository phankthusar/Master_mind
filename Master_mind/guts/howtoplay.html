Mastermind

The computer will choose 4 colors and order them in a specific way, your job is to figure out what colours and which order it chose.
To do that start by filling the 4 cells on the first row with colors and click check. The computer will respond to you by giving you a black marker for each correct color and a white marker for each correct color and position. Once you get the 4 color in the correct order you win.
